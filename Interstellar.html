About the movie

Interstellar is a 2014 science fiction movie directed, co-written and co-produced by Christoper Nolan.
Among other it stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway and Jessica Chastain.

Plot

Set in a dystopian future where humanity is struggling to survive, the film follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humanity.
